Single ' ' and Double " " Quotes

Both types of quotes are used to hold together a string. A string stores a series of characters that can be both letters and numbers but must always exist inside a pair of double or single quotes. For example beacuse "My name is Siobhan and I am 28 years old" is encased in a pair of double quotes the computer reads this as a sentence, as opposed to reading each character separately. The single quote and double quote both perform the same job, you just have to be consistent.
